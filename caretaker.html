  • What Is a Healthcare Attendant?

    A healthcare attendant also known as a patient care assistant or nursing attendant is a trained caregiver who supports individuals in their daily routines, especially the elderly, bedridden, or those recovering from major illness or surgery. Unlike nurses who perform clinical procedures, attendants focus on personal care, comfort, and emotional support, making them a vital part of in-home recovery and long-term wellness.
